Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2026 | Effective Date: May 2026

💊 MediFast is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal information when you use our platform.

1. Who We Are

MediFast Technology ("MediFast", "we", "our", "us") operates a hyperlocal healthcare delivery platform connecting patients with nearby pharmacies, verified doctors, and certified diagnostic labs across India.

Contact: jaypawar01215@gmail.com

2. Information We Collect

Personal Information:

  • Name, email address, phone number
  • Delivery address and location data
  • Date of birth and gender (optional)
  • Profile photo (optional)

Health Information:

  • Prescription details and medicine orders
  • Medical history you choose to share
  • Lab test results and reports
  • Blood group, allergies, health conditions
  • Doctor consultation notes

Technical Information:

  • Device type, browser, IP address
  • App usage patterns and preferences
  • Location data for nearby pharmacy search

3. How We Use Your Information

  • Process medicine orders and deliver to your address
  • Connect you with nearby pharmacies, doctors and labs
  • Send order status updates and notifications
  • Verify prescriptions and medicine authenticity
  • Process payments securely
  • Improve our platform and services
  • Comply with legal and regulatory requirements
  • Prevent fraud and ensure platform security

4. Data Sharing

We share your information only in these cases:

  • Pharmacy Partners: Your order details are shared with the pharmacy fulfilling your order
  • Doctors: Your appointment details shared only with your chosen doctor
  • Labs: Test booking details shared with your chosen lab
  • Payment Processors: Payment data handled by Razorpay (PCI DSS compliant)
  • Legal Requirements: If required by Indian law or court order

We NEVER sell your personal data to third parties.

5. Data Security

  • All data encrypted using AES-256 encryption
  • Secure HTTPS connections on all pages
  • Firebase security rules restrict unauthorized access
  • Prescriptions visible only to patient and fulfilling pharmacy
  • Medical records accessible only by authorized parties
  • Regular security audits conducted
  • Two-factor authentication available

6. Your Rights

Under Indian Personal Data Protection laws, you have the right to:

  • Access your personal data we hold
  • Correct inaccurate information
  • Delete your account and data
  • Withdraw consent at any time
  • Data portability — export your data
  • Opt out of marketing communications

To exercise these rights, contact: jaypawar01215@gmail.com

7. Health Data Special Protection

We treat health and medical data with the highest level of protection:

  • Prescriptions encrypted and stored securely
  • Medical history never shared without consent
  • Lab reports accessible only by patient and doctor
  • No health data used for advertising purposes
  • Compliant with Indian Medical Council guidelines

8. Cookies & Tracking

MediFast uses minimal cookies for:

  • Keeping you logged in (session cookies)
  • Remembering your preferences
  • Basic analytics to improve the app

We do not use advertising cookies or third-party tracking.

9. Data Retention

  • Account data: Retained while account is active
  • Order history: 7 years (GST compliance)
  • Medical records: 5 years (Medical Council guidelines)
  • After account deletion: Data removed within 30 days

10. Children's Privacy

MediFast does not knowingly collect data from children under 13. Parents/guardians must consent for users between 13-18 years.
